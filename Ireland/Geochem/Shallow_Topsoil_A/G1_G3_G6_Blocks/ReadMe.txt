Title	58xxxxA-65xxxxA_Shallow_Topsoil_Download_v1.2
Purpose	Multi-element geochemical data from shallow topsoil surveying in Ireland, sample media code 'A'.
Version	1.2 (replaces 1.1)
Summary	Geochemical data for analytes by pH (CaCl2), LOI (at 450°C), XRFS and ICPar analyses on <2 mm fraction deeper soil (maximum depth 0.20 m).
Description	Unique sample ID is a combination of site number and sample media code.
	Location co-ordinates data easting and northing are in Irish National Grid.
	In case of non-detects, i.e. data reported by the laboratory as "< LLD", data are censored to half the method LLD. Data that are below the method LLD but reported as being detected are included uncensored. Blank cells are null (no sample/data available).
Release notes	A batch of approximately 100 redundant samples was removed from the dataset. A batch of approximately new 100 samples was added to the dataset. Rounding of the reported values was updated. Minor edits to reported LLDs and inclusion of note on conditioned data.
Supporting documentation 	Knights, K.V. Tellus geochemical survey: shallow topsoil data from outer Dublin, the border, north, and west of Ireland . Geological Survey Ireland, 2020. 
URL	https://www.gsi.ie/en-ie/data-and-maps/Pages/Geochemistry.aspx
Authors	M. Szpak & V. Lowe. Geological Survey Ireland | Tellus
Date	March 2020
Revision Date	March 2023
Contact	tellus@gsi.ie
